Speak version 3 opens up the peak P.o.E.M.M. app to the user, allowing her to enter her own text or assemble a text by searching on a keyword, hashtag or account name on Twitter. Speak is the first app in the P.o.E.M.M.s (Poem for Excitable [Mobile] Media) series. Please visit www.poemm.net for more info.
